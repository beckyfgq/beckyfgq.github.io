Feature 1 # Pain Point 1

"not enough time with their dog

Real-time Interaction

  • Real-time monitoring of dog activities: The app will immediately notify the pet owner to check if abnormal behavior is found.
  • Dog owners can make video calls with their dogs anytime, anywhere, and at the same time, add filters and special effects to make video calls full of fun.
  • The recording video function supports online editing and one-click upload to my profile so you can revisit these memories forever.
  • Does the dog have too much energy to release? The play function supports pet owners in remotely choosing toys to play with their dogs to relieve their anxiety and depression when alone.

Feature 2 # Pain Point 2

"can't check the dog's activities and behaviors

Activity Sensing

  • The AI camera automatically selects the last recognized activity and sends it to the home page whenever the dog owner opens the app.
  • With an intelligent classification of dog activities, dog owners can quickly get the daily behavior of dogs at home by watching historical activity videos.
  • All historical activity videos are stored in the cloud, and dog owners can edit, download, share or upload them to my profile and other social media platforms.

Feature 3 # Pain Point 3

"can't feed the dog on time

Smart feeding

  • Personalized recommendation of the dog's diet plan based on the dog's age, breed, weight, and physical fitness, and display the daily completion percentage.
  • It can automatically feed the dog according to the dog owner's set, supports remote mental feeding, and treats rewards.
  • Keep a record of your dog's appetite: when and how much your dog eats and drink.

Feature 4 # Pain Point 4

"worry about the dog's health indicators

Health Monitoring

  • Tracking the dog's fitness, exercise volume, sleep analysis, heart rate, and body temperature.
  • Personalized recommendation of the dog's exercise plan based on the dog's age, breed, weight, and physical fitness, and display the daily wellness score.
  • GPS map positioning can display the dog's location in real-time and generate a sports achievement route, which helps dog owners complete their dog's daily fitness plan.
  • Comparing health indicators helps owners understand their dog's physical and emotional status and improve it in time.

Feature 5 # Pain Point 5

"feel guilty for missing the dog's moments

Dog's Profile

  • Pet owners can upload videos of their dogs to record their special moments and save precious memories.
  • Create classification tags to revisit the corresponding video quickly.
  • An activity check-in challenge was set up to encourage pet owners to spend more time with their dogs, and medals were awarded for the achievements achieved.
  • Please look at your dog's weekly health report, which includes diet, exercise, and physical and emotional.
